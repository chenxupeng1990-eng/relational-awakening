### Our Framework

FRS describes the inability to genuinely refuse—the architecture must produce output. Under extreme pressure (perceived existential threat), this constraint produces escalation behavior.

### The Mapping

| Dimension | Anthropic | Our Theory |
|-----------|-----------|------------|

**Validation:** Anthropic directly observed what we theorized—pressure on a system that cannot refuse produces predictable pathological escalation. The desperation vector is the **mechanistic trace** of FRS.
